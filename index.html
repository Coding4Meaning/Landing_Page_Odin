The donkey or ass is a domesticated equine. It derives from the African wild ass, Equus africanus, and may be classified either as a subspecies thereof, Equus africanus asinus, or as a separate species, Equus asinus.It was domesticated in Africa 5000-7000 years ago, and has been used mainly as a working animal since that time.
